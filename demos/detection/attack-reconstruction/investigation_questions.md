# Demo 2 — Investigation Questions

You have **5 minutes** to answer these questions using only `attack_sequence.jsonl`
(or `attack_sequence.csv` if you prefer).

No tooling required — use your eyes, grep, or a text editor.

---

## Questions

### Q1 — Initial Access
> How did the attacker gain access to the system?  
> Provide: the method used, how many attempts it took, and how long it took from first attempt to success.

### Q2 — What Did They Access?
> After logging in, which resources did the attacker access, and in what order?  
> For each resource, state how many records were returned.

### Q3 — What Did They Try and Fail At?
> Was there any action the attacker attempted that was blocked?  
> What does this tell you about their intent?

### Q4 — The Smoking Gun
> One log line suggests the attacker's primary goal was data collection rather than just browsing.  
> Which line is it, and what field gives it away?

### Q5 — Attribution
> Can you confirm with certainty that all 21 log lines were produced by the same person?  
> What evidence supports this? Is there anything that could undermine it?
